About Me

Get to know me a little

Even with a good life on the outside, I often felt a quiet heaviness inside‚Äîstress in my body, restlessness in my heart. My journey into mindfulness and life coaching began as a way to ease that discomfort, but it became something more: a path to reconnect with myself, gently and honestly.

Now, I support others in doing the same‚Äîlearning to listen inward, tend to what's been long ignored, and live with more ease, alignment, and self-trust.

"To live skillfully is to recognize the interconnection of all parts of our lives. It's to go deeper‚Äîbeyond what appears on the surface of our struggles. When one part heals, the rest awakens to expansion."

For me, this journey truly began during a time when, despite outward success in my career and lifestyle, I felt a deep inner discontent‚Äîan ache I couldn't explain. Alongside it came chronic back pain and physical tension that no amount of stretching or exercise could soothe.

Things began to shift when I immersed myself in deep somatic meditation courses. These practices helped me witness my mental patterns as they showed up in my body‚Äîhow irritation, anger, and depressive thoughts were not just emotional states, but physical ones too. Gaining awareness was powerful, but it wasn't a quick fix. Long-held mental and emotional patterns don't dissolve with insight alone.

The real transformation began when I started working with the right life coach. These weren't just venting sessions‚Äîthey were deep conversations that gave me clarity, language, and tools. I began to understand my values, purpose, and desires in a way that felt embodied and real. I learned to speak to myself differently, to intervene in my patterns in real time, and slowly, to shift how I showed up in the world.

I believe that within each of us lies an innate wisdom‚Äîa kind of inner compass. When we learn to access and trust it, it guides us toward a life that feels both true and fulfilling. My role as your life coach is to help you reconnect with that compass and build the skills to live in alignment with it. My goal isn't to create dependence‚Äîit's to empower you to become your own life coach. To know yourself deeply, to learn how to reparent the parts of you still waiting to be heard, and to live more skillfully, from the inside out.
